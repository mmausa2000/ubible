1. Psalm 23:1 — The Lord is my shepherd; I shall not want.

2. Psalm 23:2 — He makes me lie down in green pastures: He leads me beside the still waters.

3. Psalm 23:3 — He restores my soul: He leads me in paths of righteousness for His name’s sake.

4. Psalm 23:4 — Even though I walk through the valley of the shadow of death, I will fear no evil: for You are with me; Your rod and Your staff comfort me.

5. Psalm 23:5 — You prepare a table before me in the presence of my enemies: You anoint my head with oil; my cup runs over.

6. Psalm 23:6 — Surely goodness and mercy will follow me all the days of my life: and I will dwell in the house of the Lord forever.

7. Psalm 121:1 — I lift up my eyes to the hills, from where comes my help.

8. Psalm 121:2 — My help comes from the Lord, who made heaven and earth.

9. Psalm 121:3 — He will not allow your foot to be moved: He who keeps you will not slumber.

10. Psalm 121:4 — Look, He who keeps Israel will neither slumber nor sleep.

11. Psalm 121:5 — The Lord is your keeper: the Lord is your shade on your right hand.

12. Psalm 121:6 — The sun will not strike you by day, nor the moon by night.

13. Psalm 121:7 — The Lord will protect you from all evil; He will preserve your soul.

14. Psalm 121:8 — The Lord will preserve your going out and your coming in from this time forth, and forevermore.

15. Psalm 138:1 — I will praise You with my whole heart: before the gods I will sing praise to You.

16. Psalm 138:2 — I will worship toward Your holy temple, and praise Your name for Your lovingkindness and Your truth.

17. Psalm 138:3 — In the day when I cried, You answered me, and strengthened me with strength in my soul.

18. Psalm 138:4 — All the kings of the earth will praise You, O Lord, when they hear the words of Your mouth.

19. Psalm 138:5 — Yes, they will sing in the ways of the Lord: for great is the glory of the Lord.

20. Psalm 138:6 — Though the Lord is high, yet He respects the lowly: but the proud He knows from afar.

21. Psalm 138:7 — Though I walk in the midst of trouble, You will revive me: You will stretch forth Your hand against the wrath of my enemies.

22. Psalm 138:8 — The Lord will perfect that which concerns me: Your mercy, O Lord, endures forever.

23. Psalm 62:1 — Truly my soul waits silently for God: from Him comes my salvation.

24. Psalm 62:2 — He only is my rock and my salvation; He is my defense; I will not be greatly moved.

25. Psalm 62:5 — My soul, wait silently for God alone; for my expectation is from Him.

26. Psalm 62:6 — He only is my rock and my salvation: He is my defense; I will not be moved.

27. Psalm 62:7 — In God is my salvation and my glory: the rock of my strength, and my refuge, is in God.

28. Psalm 62:8 — Trust in Him at all times, people: pour out your heart before Him: God is a refuge for us.

29. Psalm 62:11 — God has spoken once; twice I have heard this: that power belongs to God.

30. Psalm 62:12 — Also to You, O Lord, belongs mercy: for You reward every man according to his work.

31. Psalm 46:1 — God is our refuge and strength, a very present help in trouble.

32. Psalm 46:2 — Therefore we will not fear, even if the earth is removed, and the mountains are carried into the midst of the sea.

33. Psalm 46:3 — Though its waters roar and are troubled, though the mountains shake with its swelling.

34. Psalm 46:4 — There is a river, the streams of which will make glad the city of God.

35. Psalm 46:5 — God is in the midst of her; she will not be moved: God will help her, and that right early.

36. Psalm 46:6 — The nations raged, the kingdoms were moved: He uttered His voice, the earth melted.

37. Psalm 46:7 — The Lord of hosts is with us; the God of Jacob is our refuge.

38. Psalm 46:10 — Be still, and know that I am God: I will be exalted among the nations, I will be exalted in the earth.

39. Psalm 117:1 — Praise the Lord, all nations: praise Him, all people.

40. Psalm 117:2 — For His merciful kindness is great toward us: and the truth of the Lord endures forever.

41. Psalm 37:1 — Do not fret because of evildoers, nor be envious of the workers of iniquity.

42. Psalm 37:3 — Trust in the Lord, and do good; dwell in the land, and feed on His faithfulness.

43. Psalm 37:4 — Delight yourself also in the Lord; and He will give you the desires of your heart.

44. Psalm 37:5 — Commit your way to the Lord; trust also in Him; and He will bring it to pass.

45. Psalm 37:7 — Rest in the Lord, and wait patiently for Him.

46. Psalm 37:23 — The steps of a good man are ordered by the Lord: and He delights in his way.

47. Psalm 37:24 — Though he falls, he will not be utterly cast down: for the Lord upholds him with His hand.

48. Psalm 37:39 — The salvation of the righteous is from the Lord: He is their strength in the time of trouble.

49. Psalm 37:40 — And the Lord will help them and deliver them: He will deliver them from the wicked, and save them, because they trust in Him.

50. Psalm 1:1 — Blessed is the man who does not walk in the counsel of the ungodly, nor stand in the path of sinners, nor sit in the seat of scoffers.
