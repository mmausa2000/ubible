1. Leviticus 19:27 — You shall not cut the hair on the sides of your head or trim the edges of your beard.

2. Leviticus 21:5 — The priests shall not shave their heads or trim the edges of their beards or cut their bodies.

3. Isaiah 7:20 — In that day the Lord will use a razor hired from beyond the Euphrates to shave the head and the hair of the legs, and to remove the beard.

4. 2 Samuel 10:4 — So Hanun took David’s servants, shaved off half of their beards, cut off their garments at the hips, and sent them away.

5. 2 Samuel 10:5 — When David was told, he sent messengers to meet them, for the men were greatly ashamed. The king said, “Stay at Jericho until your beards grow back, and then return.”

6. Ezekiel 5:1 — Son of man, take a sharp sword and use it like a barber’s razor to shave your head and your beard.

7. Psalm 133:2 — It is like precious oil poured on the head, running down on the beard—on the beard of Aaron—running down on the collar of his robe.

8. Jeremiah 48:37 — Every head is shaved and every beard cut off; hands are gashed and the waist is covered with sackcloth.

9. Ezra 9:3 — When I heard this, I tore my tunic and cloak, pulled hair from my head and beard, and sat down appalled.

10. Isaiah 50:6 — I offered my back to those who beat me, my cheeks to those who pulled out my beard; I did not hide my face from mocking and spitting.
